About Me

Dr. Avinash N Bhute

Associate Professor

Department of Computer Engineering

Dedicated, enthusiastic individual with outstanding academic, communication, organizational, and people skills and a passion for teaching with 22 years of experience. Completed Ph.D. (Computer Engineering) specialization in databases, computer vision and machine learning. As per the growth of industry and teaching practices, upgraded myself by completing Professional certification of IBM Hadoop, Spark Developer, IBM DB2, IBM Tivoli Directory Server V6.1, IBM RAD, Oracle9i OCA. Committed to offer interested students my full attention and expertise to help further their academic and professional goals. Exceptional track record certification success with multiple published articles, copyright and patents.
